    Centrifuge Tachometer
  • Arduino Nano 3.0
  • TCRT5000 IR Sensor
  • C/C++
  • Microcontroller System

    • This project is a tachometer used to service rotation-based medical equipment. It uses an IR Sensor to act as a line tracer which measures the rpm based on how many times a point passes through the sensor. It has a high degree of accuracy in low rpm ratings but has a bit of significant margin of error of ±300 at ratings above 15000rpm.
    This was a remote commission and i was only tasked to program the microcontroller and make the schematics for the prototype. As such, optimization for the actual usage was proven to be difficult due to limitations on hardware testing.
